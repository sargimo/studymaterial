About me

I'm Geoff, I'm a designer looking to take my skills to the next level and learn as much as I can from Udacity.

I recently moved on from my previous role that cemented my love for this industry. I was a Web Editor, Designer and Programme Specialist for the Waimakariri District Libraries. The position allowed me to dip my toes into the world of websites and I fell in love with figuring out how to incorporate creativity into the X's and O's of website design.

I never thought of coding to be such a creative outlet until I tried it, now there is no turning back!

I am confident that my eye for design and my experience in that field, incorporated with the skills I am learning here will lead me to become a full package for website design, and who knows where it may take me in the future.

All I know is that I am excited to get there.
